InkFlow AI: Text to Handwriting

Last Updated: May 6, 2026

Welcome to InkFlow AI. Your privacy is not just a feature; it is our foundational principle. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle data within the InkFlow application to ensure the absolute safety of our users and their content.

🛡️1. Privacy-First Architecture

InkFlow operates on a Local-First architecture. We believe that your handwriting and your words are private. By default:

  • On-Device Rendering: Standard AI handwriting synthesis and PDF generation happen locally using your device's hardware.
  • No Account Required: You can use the core features of InkFlow without providing any personal identifiers.

☁️2. Optional AI Cloud Services

To provide industry-leading features, certain optional components utilize secure cloud processing. These are clearly marked and only activated with your consent:

  • AI Assistant (OpenRouter): If you use the "AI Homework Assistant," your text prompts are sent to secure servers for generation. No personal data is attached to these prompts.
  • Handwriting Cloning (Replicate): If you choose to clone your own handwriting, uploaded images are processed securely to create your digital style and are then handled according to strict data-retention policies.
  • InkStore: Browsing and downloading new assets (fonts/papers) requires a secure connection to our asset delivery network.

📊2. Data Handling & Collection

We do not collect personal data. The only data interactions that occur are facilitated by trusted industry-standard providers for specific app functionalities:

  • Google Play Billing: Used securely to process premium upgrades. We never see your credit card or financial details.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements to users on the free tier. AdMob may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads.
  • Google ML Kit: Used locally for high-performance OCR (Text Recognition). No data is sent to the cloud during this process.

📱3. Permissions & Usage

To provide a seamless experience, InkFlow requests the following permissions:

  • Storage/Media: To save your generated PDF files and images to your device so you can share or print them.
  • Camera: Strictly for the OCR feature, allowing you to convert physical notes into digital text.
  • Internet: Required only for verifying premium licenses and fetching advertisements.

⚖️4. Forensic Integrity & Ethics

To protect the app's owner and maintain the integrity of our technology, every document generated by InkFlow contains a non-invasive Forensic Integrity Marker. This identifies the document as "AI-Generated" to prevent misuse in fraudulent contexts, while ensuring no user-specific data is embedded.

🛡️5. User Safety & Protection

Since InkFlow does not store your data on any server, your information is protected from server-side data breaches. Your data is as secure as your physical device. We recommend using device-level encryption and biometric locks for maximum safety.

Questions or Support?
If you have any inquiries regarding this policy or your privacy while using InkFlow, please contact us through the official support channel provided in the Google Play Store listing.
